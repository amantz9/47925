About the Dojo

We have enough room to do Sparring and Self Defense, Kata, and Weapons comfortably with mirrors in 5 FULL SIZED PERMANENT Rings. We also have 6 hanging bags, RUBBER FLOORS, a room for consultation and counseling, as well as a room with about a 12 inch thick foam floor for beginners to learn to fall properly without fear of hurting themselves. We also have a Knife Throwing, and Zen Archery Range as well as seperate Men's and Women's rest rooms with Lockers.

Class Info

Classes are $99 per month with no contract. Students receive a free uniform.

Kids 4 years and older meet Tuesdays and Thursdays from 5pm to 6pm, and 6pm to 7pm. There is no extra charge to attend both classes.

Adult only classes meet Tuesdays and Thursdays from 7pm to 8pm. Veterans get 3 months free!
